# Researches and published work

I'd like first to **clarify** what I mean by **publicated work**.
While the word _"publications"_ is commonly owned to refer any published research paper (either at a magazine, a conference or similar);
here I refer to my publications as any of my work which was written and is open to the public.

My publications are displayed in different categories:

- **Thesis**: Research work I performed at the end of any study (which is involved with a diploma). Each thesis is summarized in English and a link to the original paper is provided.
